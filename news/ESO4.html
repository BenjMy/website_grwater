Welcome students at ICA to showcase research activity

by Hector Nieto | 2025/03/12

As part of our public dissemination activities we showcased our research activities to a few secondary school students (Programme 4º ESO + Empresa) of Madrid.

Among other colleagues of Tech4Agro, we introduced them the value of geophysics for monitoring sub-soil physical processes as well as the interest of thermal infrared satellite to estimate evapotranspiration and water stress.
